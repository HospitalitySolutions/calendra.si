Platform subscription billing on register signup

Backend changes:
- Added PlatformSubscriptionBillingService to create/update platform-admin billing data when a tenant registers through the register flow.
- The service resolves the main Platform Admin tenant and creates/updates a ClientCompany payee from the signup billing details, then links a platform client to that company payee.
- Ensures the six platform subscription transaction services exist only once on the Platform Admin tenant:
  - BASICMONTHLY: Basic Package - Monthly, no VAT, 18.90 gross/net monthly equivalent
  - BASICANNUAL: Basic Package - Annual, no VAT, 18.90 gross/net monthly equivalent
  - PROMONTHLY: Pro Package - Monthly, no VAT, 34.90 gross/net monthly equivalent
  - PROANNUAL: Pro Package - Annual, no VAT, 29.67 gross/net monthly equivalent
  - BUSINESSMONTHLY: Business Package - Monthly, no VAT, 59.90 gross/net monthly equivalent
  - BUSINESSANNUAL: Business Package - Annual, no VAT, 50.92 gross/net monthly equivalent
- Uses the platform register price catalog where available, while keeping the requested annual monthly-equivalent calculation for Pro/Business and the requested Basic Annual setup.
- Creates one idempotent open bill on the Platform Admin tenant per registered tenant, with the created company as the payee (batch company target).
- Adds one subscription bill item to that open bill. The item uses a daily net/gross amount calculated from the selected package period:
  - monthly: monthly gross divided by days from the billable start date to the same day next month
  - annual: annual gross (monthly-equivalent × 12) divided by days from the billable start date to the same day next year
- Basic Monthly starts accruing after a 14-day trial; until then the open bill exists with a zero accrued quantity.
- Added a daily scheduled refresh that updates open subscription bills so quantity/accrued due amount grows day by day.
- Signup billing-details save now refreshes the Platform Admin payee data and open bill with the latest company name, VAT ID, address, city, selected package, billing interval, and payment method.
- Initial tenant provisioning also creates a placeholder platform open bill from the available company/email data, then billing details can enrich it later.

Repository changes:
- Added lookup helpers for platform subscription transaction services, open bills by subscription reference, and platform ClientCompany matching by VAT/email/name.
