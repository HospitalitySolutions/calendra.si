Entitlement booking payment selection fix

Changed files:
- guest-mobile/androidApp/src/main/kotlin/si/calendra/guest/android/ui/screens/BookScreen.kt
- guest-mobile/androidApp/src/main/kotlin/si/calendra/guest/android/ui/GuestMobileRoot.kt
- guest-mobile/iosApp/GuestMobile/BookView.swift
- guest-mobile/iosApp/GuestMobile/MainTabView.swift

What changed:
1. Android guest app
   - BookLaunchRequest now carries the clicked entitlementId from Wallet → Entitlements → Izberi termin.
   - GuestMobileRoot passes the exact wallet card entitlement id when opening the Book tab.
   - BookScreen preselects payment method ENTITLEMENT and the exact clicked entitlement id during the launch flow.
   - In entitlement launch mode, the flow no longer silently falls back to card payment or another entitlement when the clicked entitlement is temporarily not matched.

2. iOS guest app
   - BookLaunchRequest now carries the clicked entitlementId.
   - MainTabView passes entitlement.entitlementId when opening booking from Wallet → Entitlements.
   - BookView preselects the exact clicked entitlement and keeps ENTITLEMENT as the active payment method for that launch flow.
   - The selected entitlement no longer falls back to a different first matching card when a specific clicked entitlement id was provided.

Result:
- When the user taps Izberi termin on a specific entitlement/card/visit, the Payment & review step opens with "Uporabi karto ali obisk" selected and bound to that exact entitlement.
- If that exact entitlement is not valid for the resolved service, the app will not silently switch to another payment method or a different card.
