Updated folio/invoice PDF payment footer rendering.

Changes made:
1. When multiple payment methods exist, the payment lines now render downward, one below another, starting from the Payment anchor field.
2. Footer items positioned below the Payment field in the right-side payment column are now automatically pushed downward by the extra height created by additional payment lines.
3. The same downward offset is also applied to right-side elements below the payment block such as the payment QR and any other right-column footer elements, preventing overlap.
4. This preserves the existing layout for single-payment invoices and only adds vertical shifting when multiple payment lines are present.
